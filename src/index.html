Literary Evenings: Listen to the Authors of the Latest Novels

Our unique atmosphere is not only about the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and shelves filled with books but also about the special literary events we organize for our guests. We regularly invite the authors of the latest bestsellers, who personally read excerpts from their novels, sharing the stories and inspirations behind their books. It’s a perfect opportunity to meet your favorite writers, get an autograph, and immerse yourself in the literary world alongside fellow book lovers.
